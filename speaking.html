FAQ

Common Booking Questions

What languages do you speak in?

English, Mandarin, or Bahasa Malaysia — at native fluency level. For mixed audiences across Southeast Asia, I can code-switch fluidly throughout the talk if that fits your audience better.

Do you do virtual events?

Yes — keynotes, workshops, panels, and fireside chats via Zoom, Teams, or any platform you prefer. Virtual events typically come at a lower investment than in-person and can reach broader audiences. I bring the same level of preparation and engagement either way.

How far in advance should I book?

Most events are confirmed 60–90 days out. Earlier is better for keynotes at large conferences, where slides, customization, and pre-event briefings need lead time. For smaller workshops or fireside chats, shorter notice can sometimes be accommodated — just ask.

Do you travel internationally?

Yes. I'm based in Kuala Lumpur and speak frequently across Southeast Asia — Malaysia, Singapore, Thailand, Indonesia, Vietnam, Philippines. Events in other regions (Greater China, Australia, etc.) are available by arrangement.

Can you customize a talk for my audience?

Always. Every talk is tailored to your event's theme, your audience's experience level, and the specific outcomes you want them to leave with. The signature talks are starting points, not finished products. Tell me your context and I'll propose a customized angle.

What's the booking process like?

Three steps. First, send me a message via WhatsApp or the contact form with your event details (date, audience, theme, format). Second, I'll respond within 48 hours with availability and an initial proposal. Third, we have a 30-minute briefing call before confirming, so we're aligned on what success looks like.
