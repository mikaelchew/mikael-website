# Book Launch Campaign Plan
## 《直銷孫子兵法之不戰而勝》 — "The Art of War for Direct Selling"
## Target Launch: July-August 2026 (3-4 months from now)

---

## LAUNCH STRATEGY OVERVIEW

The book launch is not a single event — it's a 12-week campaign divided into three phases:

1. **Pre-Launch** (Weeks 1-6): Build anticipation, grow email list, establish authority
2. **Launch Week** (Week 7): Maximum visibility, multi-channel push
3. **Post-Launch** (Weeks 8-12): Sustain momentum, gather social proof, secondary pushes

---

## PHASE 1: PRE-LAUNCH (Weeks 1-6)

### Week 1-2: Foundation
- [ ] Finalize manuscript and submit to publisher/printer
- [ ] Get ISBN number assigned
- [ ] Commission professional book cover design (if not done)
- [ ] Set up book sales page on mikaelchew.com (upgrade from current "Coming Soon")
- [ ] Set up pre-order mechanism (if self-publishing: Gumroad, Shopify, or direct order form)
- [ ] Create a dedicated landing page for book launch email list
- [ ] Design book launch visual assets (social media graphics, email headers, web banners)

### Week 3-4: Teaser Campaign
- [ ] Announce the book launch date on all social media platforms
- [ ] Start "Behind the Book" content series (why you wrote it, the process, key stories)
- [ ] Release a short video (2-3 min) explaining what the book is about and who it's for
- [ ] Share 1 excerpt/insight from the book per week on social media (in English)
- [ ] Begin outreach to potential endorsers (DS industry leaders, business authors, mentors)
- [ ] Reach out to podcast hosts for guest appearances timed around launch
- [ ] Reach out to DS industry media/blogs for review copies

### Week 5-6: Momentum Building
- [ ] Increase book-related social content to 3-4 posts per week
- [ ] Share endorsement quotes as they come in (design as quote graphics)
- [ ] Release Chapter 1 preview (already have PDF — promote it more aggressively)
- [ ] Host a live session (IG Live, Facebook Live, or LinkedIn Live) discussing one key concept from the book
- [ ] Create a "Book Launch Team" — recruit 20-30 supporters who will buy, review, and share on launch day
- [ ] Send advance review copies (ARCs) to key influencers and reviewers
- [ ] Prepare launch week content calendar (draft all social posts in advance)
- [ ] Set up Amazon listing (if distributing via Amazon)

---

## PHASE 2: LAUNCH WEEK (Week 7)

### Day 1 (Launch Day)
- [ ] Publish "The book is HERE" announcement on all platforms
- [ ] Send email blast to full email list (newsletter + book-chapter subscribers)
- [ ] Post launch video (you holding the book, explaining what it means to you)
- [ ] Activate Book Launch Team — they all post/share/review on Day 1
- [ ] Update website: book page goes live with purchase links
- [ ] Pin launch post on all social profiles

### Day 2-3
- [ ] Share first reader reactions and screenshots
- [ ] Post a "thank you" video or message for early supporters
- [ ] Run a "First 100 buyers" or "Launch week special" promotion (signed copy, bonus chapter, exclusive Q&A access)
- [ ] Cross-post to relevant Facebook Groups and LinkedIn communities (where allowed)

### Day 4-5
- [ ] Share a specific chapter insight (teaser for those still deciding)
- [ ] Address common questions: "Is this book for me?" post
- [ ] Host a live Q&A session about the book's key concepts
- [ ] Publish YouTube/Podcast episode dedicated to the book launch

### Day 6-7
- [ ] Final "launch week" push — "Last chance for the launch special"
- [ ] Share a roundup of reviews and endorsements
- [ ] Thank your community and share what's coming next

---

## PHASE 3: POST-LAUNCH (Weeks 8-12)

### Ongoing Activities
- [ ] Continue sharing book insights weekly on social media (1-2 posts per week)
- [ ] Collect and publish reader reviews (Amazon, social media screenshots, video testimonials)
- [ ] Guest appearances on podcasts and live shows (scheduled during pre-launch)
- [ ] Write 2-3 new blog posts that tie into book concepts (drives traffic to book page)
- [ ] Consider a "Book Club" format — discuss one chapter per week on social or live
- [ ] Explore speaking engagements at DS industry events (use book as credential)
- [ ] Plan Chinese-language marketing push (since book is in Traditional Chinese)
- [ ] Explore translation/English edition for broader market

### Secondary Launch Pushes
- [ ] Week 9: "Reviews are in" compilation post
- [ ] Week 10: Holiday/event tie-in (if applicable)
- [ ] Week 11: "If you missed launch week" reminder campaign
- [ ] Week 12: Announce what's next (coaching, masterclass, speaking, Season 2 of podcast)

## DISTRIBUTION STRATEGY

### Primary Sales Channel
- Direct sales via mikaelchew.com (highest margin, captures customer data)
- Set up with Gumroad, Shopify, or WooCommerce for digital/physical orders

### Secondary Channels
- Amazon (KDP for print-on-demand, or direct upload)
- Regional bookstores in Malaysia (approach MPH, Kinokuniya, Popular)
- Bulk orders for DS companies/teams who want to use it as training material
- Speaking events (sell at back of room)

### Pricing Strategy
- Physical book: RM 79-99 (premium positioning — this is a strategic guide, not a pamphlet)
- Digital/eBook: RM 39-49
- Bundle (physical + digital): RM 99-119
- Launch week special: 10-15% discount or bonus content
