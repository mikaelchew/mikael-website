# YouTube / Podcast Series Launch Plan
## "The Long Game" by Mikael Chew

---

## CONCEPT

**Show name**: The Long Game (working title — alternatives: "Win the Right Way," "Strategy Over Hustle," "The Direct Selling Strategist")

**Format**: Solo commentary / teaching episodes. Mikael records in front of his computer (webcam-style). No guests needed initially (can add guest interviews in Season 2).

**Length**: 15-25 minutes per episode (sweet spot for YouTube + podcast)

**Frequency**: Weekly (every Tuesday or Thursday — pick a consistent day)

**Distribution**:
1. Record as video (webcam + screen share when showing frameworks)
2. Upload to YouTube as the primary video platform
3. Extract audio and distribute to podcast platforms (Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts) via a hosting service

**Positioning**: The show for direct selling leaders who want strategic depth, not motivational fluff. Each episode teaches one concept, framework, or lesson from Mikael's 23 years.

## PRODUCTION SETUP

### Equipment Needed
- Good webcam (1080p minimum, 4K preferred) or use laptop camera
- External microphone (USB condenser mic like Blue Yeti, Rode NT-USB, or Audio-Technica AT2020USB+)
- Ring light or desk lamp for good lighting
- Clean, professional background (bookshelf, minimalist wall, or virtual background with brand colors)

### Recording Software
- OBS Studio (free) or Riverside.fm (for higher quality audio/video)
- Screen recording for framework slides (OBS handles this)

### Editing & Post-Production
- Video editing: CapCut (free), DaVinci Resolve (free), or hire an editor
- Audio extraction: Export audio track from video editor
- Thumbnail creation: Canva with brand templates (dark background, Mikael's face, episode title in brand fonts)

### Podcast Hosting
- Recommended: Buzzsprout, Podbean, or Anchor (Spotify for Podcasters)
- These auto-distribute to Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Google Podcasts, Amazon Music

### YouTube Optimization
- Custom thumbnails (consistent design: Mikael's face + episode title + brand colors)
- SEO titles: "Direct Selling Strategy: [Episode Topic] | The Long Game Ep. [#]"
- Description: Episode summary + timestamps + links to blog/website/book
- Tags: direct selling, network marketing, MLM strategy, leadership, Sun Tzu, etc.
- End screen: Subscribe + next episode + playlist
- Cards: Link to relevant blog post or book page

## GROWTH STRATEGY

### Month 1-2: Foundation
- Focus on consistency (weekly episodes, no gaps)
- Cross-promote every episode on all 4 social platforms
- Embed YouTube videos in blog posts where relevant

### Month 3-4: Expansion
- Pitch yourself as a guest on other DS/leadership podcasts
- Invite 1-2 guest leaders for interview episodes
- Start a YouTube Shorts strategy with best clips

### Month 5+: Scale
- Consider a premium content tier (paid masterclass or membership)
- Live episode recordings or Q&A sessions
- Tie into book launch campaign
