## "Strategic Direct Selling Leaders" — Community Blueprint

**Status**: Deferred until Mikael has a core team of leaders to help moderate and manage.

---

## ACTIVATION CRITERIA (When to Start)

Launch this group ONLY when:
- [ ] You have 3-5 trusted leaders willing to co-moderate
- [ ] Your social media following has grown enough to seed the group (500+ combined engaged followers)
- [ ] You have bandwidth beyond social media + podcast + book launch
- [ ] The podcast and/or book has established your authority enough that people actively seek community

---

## CONCEPT

**Group Name**: Strategic Direct Selling Leaders (or "Win the Right Way — Strategic DS Community")

**Positioning**: A curated, quality-over-quantity community for direct selling leaders who want strategic depth. NOT a hype group. NOT a recruitment ground. A place for real strategic conversation.

**Membership**: Invite-only or application-based (3 qualifying questions to filter for serious leaders)

**Qualifying questions**:
1. How long have you been in direct selling?
2. What is your biggest strategic challenge right now?
3. Are you committed to building with integrity?

---

## CONTENT STRUCTURE (When Active)

- **Monday**: Strategic Question of the Week (posted by Mikael or moderator)
- **Wednesday**: Framework Deep-Dive (teach one concept, invite discussion)
- **Friday**: Win of the Week (members share a strategic win, not income)
- **Monthly**: Live Q&A with Mikael (30-60 minutes)
- **Ongoing**: Members ask questions, share challenges, support each other

---

## MODERATION RULES

- No company-specific recruitment posts
- No income claims or earnings screenshots
- No product promotion of any kind
- No hype language or motivational spam
- Focus on strategy, leadership, team building, and ethical practices
- Moderators have authority to remove off-topic content

---

## TEAM NEEDED

- 1 Lead Moderator (daily check-ins, approve posts, enforce rules)
- 2-3 Co-Moderators (rotate responsibilities)
- Mikael: Weekly post + monthly live session (manageable time commitment)

---

## REVISIT DATE

Revisit this plan after:
- YouTube/Podcast Season 1 is complete (approximately 3 months from launch)
- Book is published and getting traction
- At least 2-3 potential moderators have been identified
